About Me

I have been a Project Coordinator for the past 7 years with my current company. For the last 2 years I have been looking for a change but wasn't sure what would be the best option for me. I knew I wanted to have a meaningful job that I enjoyed. I went back and fourth on what I wanted to do from Project management to Data Analysis but neither of them peaked my interest. Scrolling through an add on social media I came across coding and full stack web developement. I researched web development and software engineering for hours. I would get so lost and that's all I wanted to do. I became so intrigued and interested in web development. I made a promise to myself that I would create a solid plan to change my career path. I began self teaching myself through courses on youtube, udemy, and free code camp. I searched for hours for the best bootcamps until I stumbled upon Georgia tech full stack bootcamp. I took the leap and signed up and here I am!
